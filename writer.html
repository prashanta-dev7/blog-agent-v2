The Evolution of Ethnic Bridal Wear in Modern Indian Weddings Bridal fashion is no longer about repeating traditions — it's about reimagining them. Contemporary Indian designers are stepping away from rigid heritage codes and creating pieces that speak to today's bride: someone educated, global, and seeking beauty that reflects her own story, not just her grandmother's. The shift is remarkable. Where previous generations had little choice — heavy silks, intricate zari work, the "rules" of what a bride should wear — this generation gets to decide. A bride today might choose a minimalist lehenga with unexpected fabric play. She might pair her dupatta in a completely unconventional way…
